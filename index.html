Smoking Assessment Tools

A comprehensive collection of scales and tolls to assess smoking dependence, cravings, and withdrawal symptoms for designing personalized cessation programs.

Assessment Scales

WISDM

Wisconsin Index of Smoking Dependence Motives

Dependence

Assesses 13 different motivational domains related to smoking dependence, providing a comprehensive profile of smoking habits.

Take Assessment

FTND

Fagerström Test for Nicotine Dependence

Dependence

A 6-item scale that measures physical dependence on nicotine, widely used in clinical settings to determine dependence severity.

Take Assessment

WSWS

Wisconsin Withdrawal Scale

Withdrawal

The Wisconsin Withdrawal Scale (WSWS) contains 7 factors to measure nicotine withdrawal symptoms and their intensity.

Take Assessment

MTSS

Motivation To Stop Scale

Motivation

Measures the current motivation and readiness to quit smoking, helping to determine the best approach for cessation.

Take Assessment

GBPS

Genetic and Biological Predisposition Scale

Biological

GBPS measures the smoker's biological susceptibility to nicotine dependence based on genetic and physiological factors.

Take Assessment

OCEAN

OCEAN-NEO Personality Inventory

Psychological

Characterizes the personality traits to identify psychological predispositions that may influence smoking behavior and cessation success.

Take Assessment
